Getting around

Take it from us, you don't need a car to make the most of your visit to San Francisco! There are plenty of ways to get around and explore the city, and we'll be providing a free shuttle service between the hotel and the event venue for the wedding.

  • Walk! San Francisco is only 7x7 miles, most places are easily within walking distance. Bring comfortable shoes and try to avoid the really steep hills.
  • SF Bus System (MUNI)
  • Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART)
  • ZipCar (membership required)
